Sustainability

AS IT RELATES TO THE

Bride

Molly's wedding dress is pre-loved from Knot Too Shabby in Stratford, ON.

Bridesmaids

The bridesmaids have been working diligently to thrift their dresses, wear what they have or borrow from a friend! You'll notice they are all different, but uniquely their own :)

Groomsmen

The groomsmen are renting their suits or wearing what they already own.

Rings

Our engagement ring and wedding bands come from Poags Jewelers in Strathroy, who provide ethically sourced, Canadian-made diamonds.

Venue

We found a venue with accommodations to prevent extra travelling during the wedding day.
  • We encourage you to park at Ivey Spencer prior to the wedding ceremony and take a 10 minute walk down Windermere Rd. to the chapel and back.
  • We are working with the venue to take the compost with us after the celebrations (since London is one of the last major cities that does not yet have a public compost program).
  • We chose a buffet style dinner so you can just take the food you will actually eat and hopefully prevent half your plate from being tossed.

Lack of Registry

We are asking for no gifts, please. We are hoping to start our marriage with minimal "stuff" and since we have never lived together before, we don't know what we need yet! If you feel compelled to give us something, money would be greatly appreciated and we guarantee it will be put to good use for something we will need and use in our new home.
